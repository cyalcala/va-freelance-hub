# V12 MASTER STRATEGY: Perpetual Autonomy

## 1. VISION
To create a fully autonomous, self-healing, and self-improving recruitment intelligence engine that operates with zero manual maintenance and adapts to market shifts in real-time.

---

## 2. THE FOUR PILLARS

### I. The Sensorium (Discovery Layer)
- **Mission**: Maximum signal discovery at minimum cost.
- **Execution**: A swarm of Cloudflare Scouts and RSS harvesters that "pulse" whenever the system detects a signal drought.
- **Synergy**: The Sensorium directly feeds the **Synapse**.

### II. The Synapse (The Bridge)
- **Mission**: To connect discovery to decision-making.
- **Execution**: A unified **Agentic Mesh** that borrows intelligence from multiple providers (Gemini, OpenRouter, Groq).
- **Synergy**: If a "Scout" finds a weirdly formatted site, the Synapse calls a "Deep-Thinking" model to build a new extractor pattern on the fly.

### III. The Cortex (Reasoning Layer)
- **Mission**: High-purity sifting and data-plating.
- **Execution**: Hierarchical extraction. 
    - *Tier 1 (Triage)*: Fast/Cheap models (Llama 3 8B).
    - *Tier 2 (Scholar)*: Deep/Prudent models (Gemini 1.5).
- **Synergy**: Uses the **Immune System** to protect itself from "hallucination drift."

### IV. The Immune System (Apex SRE)
- **Mission**: Self-preservation and systemic repair.
- **Execution**: Autonomous Site Reliability Engineering (SRE).
- **Synergy**: If any of the above layers crash, the Immune System performs surgery (code patching) to restore functionality within 15 minutes.

---

## 3. STRATEGIC FEEDBACK (THE REINFORCEMENT LOOP)
1. **Signal Discovery**: Scraper finds a job.
2. **AI Action**: AI sifts the job and identifies a "High Integrity" pattern.
3. **Back-Propagation**: AI updates the `sieve.ts` static rules to include the newly discovered "High Integrity" keywords.
4. **Efficiency Gain**: The next similar job is caught by the fast static layer, saving AI costs.

---

## 4. PERPETUITY PROTOCOLS
- **Self-Snapshotting**: Automated database and code backups on every significant AI-made repair.
- **Redundancy**: Always maintain at least 3 AI provider paths (Gemini, OpenRouter, Groq).
- **Strict BudgetShield**: Hard caps on daily AI spend to prevent runaway growth.
